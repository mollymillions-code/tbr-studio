## Season Arc

### Setup
Preseason preparation. Boat upgrades from last season. What changed after Monaco?
The question: Can the engineering edge be maintained? Can it be improved?

### Rising Action
New team members arrive. Old hands show them the ropes. Preparing for a two-pilot operation.
Technical challenges: adapting the RaceBird setup for two different driving styles.

### Midpoint
A race-day crisis they solve under pressure. This is the moment the engineering team becomes a story the audience cares about.

### Climax
An innovation that gives TBR a competitive edge. The moment their work directly impacts a race result.

### Resolution
The team that makes it all possible. Recognition. The faces behind the machine.

## Content Themes
- Behind-the-scenes footage (hands on the boat, late nights, problem-solving)

- Team dynamics and camaraderie
- The garage as a character (the workspace, the tools, the intensity)

- Show the precision, the detail, the craft
- These are not background characters; they are co-protagonists
- Documentary B-roll feel: close-ups on hands, tools, screens, the boat
