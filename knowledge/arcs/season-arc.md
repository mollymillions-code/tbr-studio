# TBR Season Arc — 2025

## The Two Arc Categories

This season is told through two interwoven narrative structures:

### 1. The Season Story
The macro narrative. A 10-episode documentary told through social content.
Each episode is a content wave of 5-10 pieces over 1-2 weeks.
The season story is about TBR as an entity: the team, the mission, the journey.

### 2. Per-Race Stories
The micro narratives. Each race is its own mini-movie.
Pre-race tension, race day drama, post-race reflection.
Per-race stories feed INTO the season story but have their own arc.

These two categories are woven together through three character threads:
- **JP's Thread** — The veteran's perspective
- **Masha's Thread** — The newcomer's perspective
- **The Team's Thread** — The collective perspective

## Iterative Episode Building

**CRITICAL:** Episodes 4-10 cannot be pre-written. They must be built from what ACTUALLY HAPPENED.

The agent's process for building each episode:
1. Read `/knowledge/season/timeline.md` for chronological context
2. Read the relevant race arc file (if race-related)
3. Read each character file for their current arc state
4. Read `/knowledge/intelligence/live-context.md` for real-time cultural context
5. Synthesize all of this into the episode's content plan
6. Present to human for approval before creating anything

Each episode's content should reference and build on previous episodes.
"Remember when Masha first walked into the garage? Look at her now."
Continuity is what makes this a documentary, not a series of disconnected posts.
